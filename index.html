FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

What does a partnership with MeriScope look like?
It starts with a strategy call so we can learn about your business. From there, we build a custom roadmap — usually delivered within a week. Then we roll up our sleeves and start building, typically over 4–8 weeks. We also offer ongoing retainers for teams that want continuous support and expansion.
What's the investment?
Monthly retainers range from $2,500–$5,000 depending on scope. Project work is scoped individually based on what you need. The best way to get a clear number is to book a strategy call — no pressure, just clarity.
How is this different from an AI agency?
Most agencies productize a playbook and run it at scale. We work with a handful of partners and build custom systems we've already proven on ourselves. You get direct access to the founder and solutions built specifically for your business — not a template.
I'm not technical. Is that a problem?
Not at all — that's the whole point. You bring the business expertise and the vision. We handle the technical side. We'll make sure you understand what's being built and why, without drowning you in jargon.
What happens after we launch?
We don't build it and bounce. Post-launch includes monitoring, optimization, and making sure your team feels confident running everything. From there, you can continue with an ongoing retainer or fly solo — either way, you own everything we build.
